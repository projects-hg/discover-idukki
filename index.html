🌿 Discover Idukki

Idukki is Kerala's highest district, a land of misty mountains, ancient forests, aromatic spice gardens, and warm hospitality. From the tea-carpeted hills of Munnar to the wildlife-rich shores of Periyar Lake, every corner tells a story of nature's magnificence.
